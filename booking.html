Booking Request预约申请

All bookings are subject to availability. Please submit the booking request form first. We will contact you to confirm availability, pricing and payment arrangement. 所有预约需视档期而定。请先提交预约申请表格，我们会联系你确认档期、价格与付款安排。

Payment Summary付款说明

Pet Boarding Deposit寄养定金 RM50
Pet Daycare Deposit日托定金 Not required不需要
Pet Grooming Deposit美容定金 Not required不需要

Boarding deposit is required only after availability is confirmed by The Paw Room. If the booking is cancelled, the deposit is non-refundable. 寄养定金只会在 The Paw Room 确认有档期后才需要支付。 如取消寄养预约，定金将不予退还。

For daycare, check-in starts from 9:30am and latest check-out is 8:00pm. Daycare is capped at 9 hours. Any stay above 9 hours will be charged as boarding. 日托 check-in 时间为早上 9:30 起，最迟 check-out 时间为晚上 8:00。 日托最多 9 小时，超过 9 小时将按寄养费用计算。
