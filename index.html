Booking Terms预约条规

To keep every pet safe, comfortable and properly cared for, all pets must meet the following requirements before boarding or daycare is accepted. 为了确保每只宠物安全、舒适并获得合适照顾， 所有狗狗在接受寄养或日托前都必须符合以下要求。

Requirements基本要求

Health & safety requirements 健康与安全要求

  • Dogs must have completed their required vaccinations.狗狗必须已完成所需疫苗。
  • Dogs must be free from skin disease.狗狗不可患有皮肤病。
  • Dogs must be free from fleas and ticks.狗狗不可有跳蚤或壁虱。
  • Dogs must be free from contagious health conditions.狗狗不可有传染性健康问题。
  • Dogs with aggressive behaviour, frequent barking, or a history of biting people or dogs may not be accepted.有攻击行为、频繁吠叫，或曾咬人 / 咬狗的狗狗可能不被接受。
  • Owners are required to provide the dog’s daily food and personal items.主人需提供狗狗日常食物与个人用品。
  • Female dogs in heat must wear diapers, provided by the owner.发情期母狗必须穿尿片，尿片需由主人提供。
  • Male dogs are required to wear diapers during boarding or daycare. Diapers must be provided by the pet owner.公狗在寄养或日托期间需穿尿片，尿片需由主人提供。
Owner Responsibility主人责任

Disclosure & special care 资料告知与特别照顾

  • If your dog requires special attention, including food allergies, health conditions or behavioural habits, the owner must inform The Paw Room in advance.如果狗狗有食物敏感、健康状况或行为习惯等需要特别注意的事项，主人必须提前告知 The Paw Room。
  • This allows us to record the information properly and provide suitable care.这样我们才能记录资料并提供更合适的照顾。
  • If the owner fails to disclose important information and the dog becomes injured, unwell or unsuitable for boarding, the related responsibility will be borne by the owner.如主人未提前告知重要资料，导致狗狗受伤、不适或不适合继续寄养，相关责任将由主人承担。
  • If a dog shows aggressive or severe stress-related behaviour during boarding, The Paw Room will contact the owner and may require early pick-up.如狗狗在寄养期间出现攻击性或严重压力反应，The Paw Room 会联系主人，并可能要求提前接回。
  • Fees paid for the affected booking period are non-refundable if early pick-up is required due to safety or behaviour concerns.如因安全或行为问题需要提前接回，已支付的相关预约费用将不予退款。
  • If a dog becomes unwell during boarding or daycare, The Paw Room will contact the owner immediately. In serious cases, the dog may be sent to a veterinary clinic, and all related costs will be borne by the owner.如狗狗在寄养或日托期间身体不适，The Paw Room 会立即联系主人。严重情况下可能会送往兽医诊所，相关费用由主人承担。
The Paw Room reserves the right to decline any pet that does not meet boarding requirements or may present a safety risk, without further explanation. The Paw Room 保留拒绝任何不符合寄养要求或可能造成安全风险的宠物之权利，恕不另行解释。
