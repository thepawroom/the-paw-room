Booking Terms
Boarding Requirements

To keep every pet safe, comfortable and properly cared for, all pets must meet the following requirements before boarding or daycare is accepted.

Requirements

Health & safety requirements

  • Dogs must have completed their required vaccinations.
  • Dogs must be free from skin disease.
  • Dogs must be free from fleas and ticks.
  • Dogs must be free from contagious health conditions.
  • Dogs with aggressive behaviour, frequent barking, or a history of biting people or dogs may not be accepted.
  • Owners are required to provide the dog’s daily food and personal items.
  • Female dogs in heat must wear diapers, provided by the owner.
Owner Responsibility

Disclosure & special care

  • If your dog requires special attention, including food allergies, health conditions or behavioural habits, the owner must inform The Paw Room in advance.
  • This allows us to record the information properly and provide suitable care.
  • If the owner fails to disclose important information and the dog becomes injured, unwell or unsuitable for boarding, the related responsibility will be borne by the owner.
  • If a dog shows aggressive or severe stress-related behaviour during boarding, The Paw Room will contact the owner and may require early pick-up.
  • Fees paid for the affected booking period are non-refundable if early pick-up is required due to safety or behaviour concerns.
  • If a dog becomes unwell during boarding or daycare, The Paw Room will contact the owner immediately. In serious cases, the dog may be sent to a veterinary clinic, and all related costs will be borne by the owner.
The Paw Room reserves the right to decline any pet that does not meet boarding requirements or may present a safety risk, without further explanation.
