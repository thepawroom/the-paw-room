Boarding Check-Out Policy 寄养 Check-Out 说明

Check-out time for pet boarding is before 12:00 PM.

Late check-out between 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM will be charged at RM10 per hour.

Check-out after 5:00 PM may be charged as a full daycare session or additional boarding day.

This allows proper room preparation and scheduling for incoming pets. 寄养 check-out 时间为中午 12:00 前。

Important Note重要说明

Limited large-room availability 大型犬房间名额有限

Pets above 15kg require availability confirmation before booking is accepted, as limited large-room space is available. 15kg 以上狗狗因需使用较大空间，名额有限。请先提交预约申请， 我们会确认档期、房间安排与狗狗情况后，再正式确认预约。

Deposit Policy定金说明

Boarding deposit only 仅寄养需要定金

Pet Boarding requires a RM50 deposit to reserve the slot. If the boarding booking is cancelled, the deposit is non-refundable. 宠物寄养需要支付 RM50 定金以保留位置。如取消寄养预约，定金将不予退还。

Full payment is required upon check-in. Daycare and Grooming do not require deposit. Check-in 时需支付全额费用。宠物日托与宠物美容不需要支付定金。
